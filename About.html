Our Story

Crafting Luxury,
Defining Style

Since 2012, LUXE has been at the intersection of heritage craftsmanship and contemporary fashion — creating pieces that transcend trends and become lifelong companions.

The Beginning

Born from a
Passion for Excellence

LUXE was founded in a small atelier in Mumbai by designer Ananya Mehta, who believed that true luxury is not about price — it's about the feeling a garment gives you when you wear it. Every stitch, every fabric choice, every silhouette is considered with obsessive care.

Over a decade later, we've grown into a global brand serving discerning customers across 40+ countries, while staying true to our founding philosophy: exceptional quality and timeless design for those who truly appreciate them.
